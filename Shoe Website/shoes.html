About us

Nike Company

Founded: January 25, 1964 (as Blue Ribbon Sports), renamed Nike, Inc. in 1971.

Founders: Bill Bowerman (a track coach) and Phil Knight (a student-athlete).

Headquarters: Beaverton, Oregon, USA.

Logo: The iconic Swoosh, designed by Carolyn Davidson in 1971.
